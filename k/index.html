9月14日

这是我来到滴水湖的第六个周末，早上一口气绕滴水湖🏃了4公里，大致半圈。只要心率不超过175次/分，我似乎就能一直跑下去。

在网上看到了一位历史学家对Why do the Chinese not care about the lack of freedom？的回答，觉得在理，摘录如下：

The Chinese do not worship the Statue of Liberty. Liberalism is the core value of the West, but not the core value of China, The Chinese are more concerned about fairness. Freedom and fairness are contradictory. This is the biggest cultural difference between China and the West.
中国人并不崇拜自由女神。自由主义是西方的核心价值观，但不是中国的核心价值观。中国人更关注公平。自由与公平是相互矛盾的。这是中国与西方之间最大的文化差异。

Liberalism comes from the law of the jungle, which always benefits the strong. The United States has 11 aircraft carriers, so they advocate free navigation. Western countries have mastered mainstream media and Internet platforms, so they advocate freedom of speech. The West has the largest Financial institution, so they advocate free trade. But this is not fair to other small contries, and they cannot win the competition.
自由主义起源于丛林法则，而丛林法则总是有利于强者。美国拥有 11 艘航空母舰，所以它倡导“航行自由”。西方国家掌控主流媒体和互联网平台，所以他们倡导“言论自由”。西方拥有最大的金融机构，所以他们倡导“自由贸易”。但这对小国来说并不公平，因为他们无法在竞争中获胜。

Excessive free market policies have made wealth more concentrated in a few people. Excessive freedom of speech has given a small number of extreme political propaganda, hate speech and fake news gain greater influence. Excessive political freedom has allowed vested interest groups to kidnap policies. These will make society unfair, make the lives of ordinary people more difficult, and even make the world more chaotic and dangerous.
过度的自由市场政策让财富更加集中于少数人手中。过度的言论自由让少数极端的政治宣传、仇恨言论和虚假新闻获得更大的影响力。过度的政治自由使既得利益集团能够绑架政策。这些趋势会让社会更加不公平，让普通人的生活更加艰难，甚至让世界更加混乱和危险。

Confucianism believes that only by establishing a fair order can the world be prosperous and peaceful. So China’s political system has adopted the fairest examination system in the world, not the Western voting system.
儒家认为，只有建立公平的秩序，世界才能繁荣与和平。因此，中国的政治制度采取了世界上最公平的考试制度，而不是西方的投票制度。

投稿状态从Awaiting assignment to Executive Editor到Awaiting assigment to Associate Editor四天之后被JAE拒了。AE认为我的文章以不仅仅聚焦于应用生态学的期刊为目标可能更好，理由是：

My decision to redirect your work reflects its aims and content. Although your manuscript presents a very interesting and creative piece of research, it does not yet provide recommendations that can inform the management of the natural environment. My impression is that further steps are needed before this topic can fully contribute to the management sphere.

在科学史上，有人提出了观点，另一些人对此进行了验证的案例不胜枚举。我倾向于认为是我对所选择的两位审稿人的推理由写了Our work is an extension of this work，并附上了DOI，而我提供了DIO号的这两篇文章是发表在计算机应用相关的期刊上的，尽管我是从生态应用角度进行写作的。AE的观点当然没有问题，但后续我还是会另投生态应用的期刊。只是，我需要修改下Cover Letter的措辞了。

这件事情给我的启示在于：对于推荐审稿人，我之前的考虑还不够周全。
